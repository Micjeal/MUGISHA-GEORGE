About Our Tailoring Enterprise

Founded by Mr Mugisha George, our family-run tailoring business has been serving Kabwohe Town and surrounding communities with quality craftsmanship for over 15 years. We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

As an authorized distributor of Nytil products, we offer premium fabrics and materials for all your tailoring needs. Our skilled tailors combine traditional techniques with modern designs to create garments that stand the test of time.
